# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Writers Factory is committed to providing a welcoming, respectful, and collaborative environment for all contributors, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

We believe that creativity and engineering both thrive in environments of mutual respect, constructive feedback, and open-minded exploration.

## Expected Behavior

All participants in the Writers Factory community are expected to:

- **Be respectful and constructive** in all communications
- **Welcome diverse viewpoints** and creative approaches
- **Provide helpful, actionable feedback** rather than criticism
- **Support a culture of curiosity** and experimentation
- **Assume good intentions** when interpreting others' contributions
- **Respect boundaries** between constructive critique and personal attacks
- **Acknowledge contributions** from others generously

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- **Harassment or intimidation** of any kind
- **Hate speech** or discriminatory language/actions
- **Personal attacks** on contributors or users
- **Disruptive or aggressive behavior** in discussions or reviews
- **Publishing others' private information** without consent
- **Trolling, insulting comments,** or deliberate inflammatory behavior
- **Unwelcome sexual attention** or advances

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub repository, issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Community communications (email, chat, forums)
- Official project events
- Public representation of the project

## Enforcement

### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

- Opening a private issue on GitHub (if possible)
- Contacting the project maintainer directly
- Using GitHub's reporting features

All reports will be reviewed and investigated confidentially.

### Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in:

1. **Warning**: Private conversation about the behavior
2. **Temporary Ban**: Temporary removal from community spaces
3. **Permanent Ban**: Permanent removal from all project spaces

The severity of consequences depends on:
- Nature and severity of the violation
- Whether it was a first offense or repeat behavior
- Response to feedback and willingness to correct behavior

## Questions?

If you have questions about this Code of Conduct, please open an issue or contact the maintainers.
